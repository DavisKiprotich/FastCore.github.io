Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1Introduction

FastCore provides fasting, hydration, weight, mood, and wellness tracking tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the app and related services.

By using FastCore, you acknowledge the practices described in this policy.

2Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and profile information such as email address, display name, profile photo, and Google sign-in details when you create or connect an account.
  • Wellness information such as fasting sessions, weight entries, hydration logs, mood selections, questionnaire answers, goals, schedules, and notification preferences.
  • Technical information such as app version, device and operating system details, and diagnostic information made available by the platform or our service providers.
  • Subscription metadata such as subscription tier, status, product identifier, expiration date, and store-related IDs from Apple, Google, or RevenueCat.

Payment cards and full billing credentials are processed by the app stores and are not stored by FastCore.

3How We Use Information

  • Provide app features, reminders, and account access.
  • Sync your history and preferences across devices when you sign in.
  • Manage subscriptions, trial access, purchases, and customer support.
  • Improve app performance, stability, language support, and security.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and comply with legal obligations.

4Storage, Sync, and Security

Some data is stored locally on your device so the app can work quickly and support guest mode.

If you sign in, certain account and tracking records may also be stored in Supabase so your information can sync across devices.

  • Authentication and synced profile data may be processed through Supabase.
  • Subscription status may be processed through RevenueCat and the Apple App Store or Google Play.
  • We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Using guest mode keeps more of your information on your device, but uninstalling the app can remove local-only records.

5Notifications

With your permission, FastCore may schedule notifications such as:

  • Fasting reminders and eating-window alerts.
  • Hydration reminders.
  • Weight check-ins.
  • Motivational and progress notifications.

6When We Share Information

We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate FastCore.

  • Supabase for authentication and synced data storage.
  • RevenueCat and app stores for subscription and billing workflows.
  • Expo and platform services for notifications, distribution, and app infrastructure.

We do not sell your personal information.

7Your Choices and Rights

  • You can review and update much of your information inside the app.
  • You can disable notifications in FastCore or in your device settings.
  • You can sign out and use guest mode if you do not want cloud sync.
  • If you are signed in, you can use the in-app Delete Account option to permanently delete your FastCore account and synced data.
  • You can still contact us if you need help with a synced data deletion request.
  • You can remove guest-mode or other local-only data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • Deleting your FastCore account does not cancel subscriptions billed through the app stores; manage or cancel those in the relevant store settings.

8Retention and Deletion

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain subscriptions, resolve disputes, protect the app, and meet legal obligations.

  • Local records remain on your device until you clear the app data or uninstall.
  • If you delete your account from inside the app while signed in, we delete your FastCore account and synced wellness records from our active systems, except where limited retention is required for legal, billing, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.
  • Billing and subscription records may be retained by the app stores and RevenueCat under their own policies.

9Children's Privacy

FastCore is a wellness app for people who are able to make their own health and account decisions. It is not intended for children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through FastCore, contact us at devmile45@gmail.com so we can review and respond.

10Changes and Contact

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will appear on this page with a revised last-updated date.

If you have questions, support requests, or a data deletion request, email devmile45@gmail.com.
